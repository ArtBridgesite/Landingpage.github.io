Our Value Proposition

ArtBridge brings the popular physical craft store experience online. We provide bundled DIY toolkits, raw materials, and detailed tutorials to help you save on costs and enjoy a flexible crafting experience.

Understanding Your Needs

For Consumers

  • Experience affordable online crafting sessions
  • Purchase curated DIY kits including tools, materials, and tutorials
  • Learn new crafts anytime, anywhere

Pain Points

  • High costs of physical craft stores
  • Inconvenient scheduling and location limitations
  • Lack of affordable DIY options

Expected Gains

  • Cost-effective online crafting experience
  • Flexible and convenient learning environment
  • High-quality DIY kits with expert guidance
  • For merchants: Reduced overhead and broader customer reach

Why Choose ArtBridge?

For Consumers

Enjoy an interactive crafting experience at home with high-quality, curated DIY kits and tutorials.

Our stakeholder interviews revealed that personalized video tutorials, real-time support, and a vibrant online community significantly enhance the overall experience. Consumers appreciate having all necessary materials bundled together, saving time and effort in sourcing quality supplies.

For Merchants & Suppliers

Reduce overhead costs by moving operations online and expand your customer base with affordable DIY kit procurement.
